Mouse Haunt Mouse Haunt is a project that contained several different products within it.
The project revolves around Mice and Ghosts, with all gamemodes tying into a complete ecosystem.
Lock'n'Load, Mouse Haunt's first gamemode, is a idle farming game, sucessfully launched in 2022.
Dodgeball, it's second gamemode, is a team-based competitive online multiplayer, where players compete in heated matches of dodgeball.
Castle Heist, the last gamemode, has the players invading a castle looking for the cursed Cheese, with a twist: players change teams when they die.
Working as Lead Economy Designer, I've help make this project a reality, working with a team of over 20 people over the course of one year, for a very passionate community of people around the world.

Find it on: Mouse Haunt Whitepaper
